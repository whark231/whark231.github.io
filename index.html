Stochastic Systems Analysis and Simulation:
War and Peace

My team and I trained a model using the entire War and Peace text to decipher any given encoded message. We found probabilities of each possible sequence of characters and modelled mappings as states in a Markov chain. Then, we used a score function to compare different mappings to determine the best mapping function.
